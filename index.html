What we do



We live in a time of great opportunity to tackle the world’s water problems. But we can only start working on solutions through collaboration. That’s why we propose a program designed to bring academia, public policy makers, entrepreneurs, communities and their leaders together to work on these problems.

Teams will work for seed funding, mentorship and networking opportunities. The teams will be responsible of taking community needs and wants and academic (and non academic) research and knowledge into consideration when working on their solutions.

The program will consist of 2 judged phases. The first will last 3 weeks. After which 5 selected teams will continue for 3 months of development and mentorship, concluding in another community led voting. Three final teams will be selected recieving seed funding and future opportunities.
